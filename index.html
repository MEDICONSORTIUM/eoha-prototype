Context Aware

Built for Africa.

Africa bears the highest burden of malaria and rapidly rising NCDs. This platform is purpose-built for this context, leveraging geospatial demographics to enable outbreak detection.

By integrating optical and radar EO data, we support evidence-based public health planning across South Africa and the continent.
